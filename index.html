Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does Ace Vet Care Kenya offer?

We provide a wide range of veterinary services including general check-ups, vaccinations, grooming, surgery, pet boarding, dental care, and emergency treatment.

2. Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, we encourage pet owners to book appointments in advance to avoid long wait times and ensure personalized care. However, we also accommodate walk-ins where possible.

3. What types of pets do you treat?

We treat dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and other small household pets. If you're unsure, feel free to contact us to confirm.

4. Are vaccinations really necessary for my pet?

Absolutely! Vaccinations protect your pet from common, life-threatening diseases like rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and more. We offer tailored vaccination plans based on your pet’s age and health.

5. Is your boarding facility safe and supervised?

Yes. Our pet boarding facility is clean, secure, and supervised by trained staff to ensure your pet is safe, fed, and comfortable while you're away.
